When a couple had a fight, the husband slipped the word, divorce, and he said it three times, “divorce, divorce, divorce.”   Would this be official?!
Imam sent you answer
02 / May / 2019 16:55:32
Any words that were imprudent or were unintentionally said during a fight are considered as telling a lie to Allah.   In such case, we have to reflect back, then prove it to Allah by our action. As a way to demonstrate our reflections, the Quran inculcates to give meals to 10 poor people. “Have the husband give 3 pounds each to 10 poor people” the teacher advised.
